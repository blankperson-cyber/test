Welcome To LISI

Laboratory of Intelligent Systems and Informatics :

LISI is a research lab at Centre Universitaire Abdelhafid Boussouf de Mila, dedicated to AI, IoT, and Optimization. Our teams focus on intelligent systems, machine learning, network security, and smart technologies, driving innovation in fields like healthcare, agriculture, and smart cities through cutting-edge research and development.
